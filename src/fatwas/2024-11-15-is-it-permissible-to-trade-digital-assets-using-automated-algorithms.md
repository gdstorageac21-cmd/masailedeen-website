question: Is it completely permissible to trade digital assets using automated
  algorithms in Islamic finance?
---
The core principles of Islamic finance require transparency and the absence of Maysir (gambling) and Gharar (uncertainty). When automated algorithms are used for trade execution, one must ensure that the algorithms do not engage in prohibited transactions.
